Instructions

To capture a deadlift video from your left side, set up your camera to the left of the barbell, ensuring a clear view of both you and the barbell. Position yourself at the barbell so your left side faces the camera, then begin recording.

Focus on maintaining proper form, especially ensuring your back and legs remain visible throughout the lift. Grip the barbell with both hands, using an overhand or mixed grip, and position your feet hip-width apart. Execute the lift by pushing through your heels, extending your hips and knees simultaneously while keeping your back straight. Lift the barbell until you are standing upright, then lower it back down under control. After completing your set, review the footage for visibility and form. Ensure that the entire lift, from setup to lockout, is captured clearly.
